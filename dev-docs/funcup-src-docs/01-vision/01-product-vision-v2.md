# ├ 01 Product Vision v2   
# **Purpose:**   
Define the long-term vision of funcup, the change the product intends to bring to the soecialty coffee world, and the role the platform aims to play in the global coffee ecosystem.   
 --- 
# 1. Vision Statement   
The platform transforms coffee from a purely physical product into a **discoverable, traceable, and experience-driven digital object…**   
```
...to become the reference system for specialty coffee experience worldwide.
```
It achieves its goal by connecting specialty coffee products, roasters and consumers into a **shared digital system** where coffee can be identified, understood, and explored.   
**funcup** focuses on the ecosystem surrounding **specialty coffee roasters **and the global community of **specialty coffee consumers**, operating within the broader framework of the **SCA-driven specialty coffee world**.   
From the consumers’ standpoint, funcup aims to become **the global coffee discovery and experience layer**.   
From the roasters’ standpoint, funcup aspires to become a **structured product data source**.   
 --- 
# 2.  The Problem   
Specialty coffee exists primarily as a **physical product with fragmented information**.   
Coffee drinkers frequently encounter problems such as:   
- not remembering coffees they previously enjoyed   
- being unable to find the same coffee again   
- not knowing how to brew a coffee optimally   
- not knowing whether a coffee will match their taste preferences   
   
At the same time, **coffee roasters lack structured feedback about how their coffees are actually experienced by consumers**.   
Most coffee-related information today is:   
- fragmented across roasters’ websites   
- limited to packaging descriptions   
- inconsistent across retailers   
- unstructured across social media   
   
As a result, the act of drinking coffee rarely becomes **structured knowledge about coffee**.   
funcup **addresses this gap** by transforming **personal coffee experiences into structured collective knowledge about coffee**.   
```
from drinking coffee —> to understanding coffee
```
 --- 
# 3. The Core Idea   
The core mechanic of funcup is simple and intuitive:   
> Scan coffee.   

Scanning a **QR code** on the coffee package transforms a **physical product into a digital coffee object**.   
From this moment, the user can:   
- rate the coffee,   
- record their tasting experience,   
- see how others experienced the same coffee,   
- learn about the coffee’s origin and production,   
- explore the roaster behind the coffee.   
   
This interaction creates a direct bridge between **drinking coffee and understanding coffee**.   
> Scan coffee → Rate coffee → Discover coffee   

 --- 
# 4. The Ecosystem funcup Connects   
funcup connects three core elements of the specialty coffee ecosystem:   
**Coffee**   
The physical product: individual coffees, labels, and lots.   
**Roasters**   
Specialty coffee roasters who define the identity of the coffee through sourcing, roasting, and storytelling.   
**Consumers**   
Coffee drinkers whose tasting experiences form a collective knowledge layer about coffee.   
Each group contributes a different type of information.   
Roasters provide:   
- product facts   
- origin information   
- roasting data   
- brewing guidance.   
   
Consumers provide:   
- tasting experiences   
- brewing behaviour   
- community feedback.   
   
Through funcup, these three elements become connected through **structured tasting and consumer data and shared discovery**.   
 --- 
# 5. Long-Term Horizon   
funcup has the necessary potential to become an important infrastructure layer in the specialty coffee ecosystem.   
In the long-term, funcup roles include:   
- global coffee discovery platform,   
- consumer tasting data layer   
- transparency interface for coffee origin   
- recommendation system for coffee preferences.   
   
Over time the platform may allow users to explore coffee through:   
- flavour preferences,   
- brewing styles,   
- origin regions,   
- roasting styles.   
   
**For consumers**   
funcup becomes a **personal history of their coffee journey** and a discovery tool helping users find coffees aligned with their taste preferences.   
**For roasters**   
funcup offers **high-precision data feedback**, providing structured insight into how their coffees are experienced by consumers worldwide.   
**For advertisers and brands**   
funcup becomes a **highly targeted marketing funnel inside the specialty coffee ecosystem**.   
 --- 
# 6. Product Philosophy   
funcup is designed around three core principles.   
### Simplicity   
The product must remain effortless to use and accessible even for users who are new to specialty coffee.   
The key interaction is simple:   
```
scan coffee
```
The act of drinking coffee should seamlessly connect to recording and understanding the experience.   
 --- 
### Credibility   
Coffee information must remain trustworthy and structured.    
This is achieved through verified roasters providing verified data about coffee lots, while consumer tasting feedback following a structured vocabulary that allows experiences to be compared and aggregated as data.   
 --- 
### Discovery   
The platform should constantly encourage exploration.   
Users should continuously discover new coffees, new roasters, new brewing approaches and new flavour experiences.   
 --- 
# 7. The Future of Coffee Discovery   
In the future, discovering coffee may become similar to discovering music.   
Just as music platforms allow users to explore artists, albums, and genres, **funcup** allows users to explore coffees, roasters, and flavor profiles.   
funcup aims to be one of the platforms enabling this shift.   
Instead of asking:   
> “What coffee should I buy?”   

Users may start asking:   
> “What coffee should I discover next?”   

Over time, the platform may build a **global taste graph for coffee**, mapping relationships between coffees, flavor preferences, and consumer taste patterns.   
This creates a new type of infrastructure for understanding how coffee is experienced around the world.   
 --- 
# 8. Guiding Principle   
The guiding principle behind funcup is:   
```
Coffee is not only a beverage.
It is an experience worth discovering.
```
funcup exists to make that discovery easier, richer, and shared.
